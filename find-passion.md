# Find the Passion
* **Description**: To explain about the, what's the passion, How to find the passion and building the passion.

* **Passion**: If you feel joy and love when you think, understand, or work for a long time, that is a passionate field for you.

* **Find the Passion**: No shortcuts for finding the passion. But using the scientific method called the J-curve or learning curve, it is possible to create a passion for a specific field. To do this, first choose a field of interest and you will feel happy at first because you are interested in this field. However, when you realize that there is a lot to learn in this field, you will feel pressured and frustrated as to whether you will be able to complete it. If you overcome this and work hard and acquire the necessary knowledge and practice, that field will become very simple and easy. This will lead to happiness in that field and a love for that field.

* **Building the Passion**: There are certain processes that need to be gone through to create passion for the chosen field of interest, which are explained here.
  * **Step-1: Trail and Error**: In this step, you will try to find your passion for your chosen area of ​​interest by trying out different fields and discovering which field brings you joy. This process is not very effective, but it is the most common process.
  * **Step-2: Hardship**: At this step, you will lose your curiosity about your chosen field and realize that there is a lot to learn in that field. This realization will create pressure and frustration about whether you can complete it. At this time, you must decide and work hard to overcome that pressure and frustration.
  * **Step-3: Avoid Overthinking**: At this step, you will realize that you have a lot of knowledge to gain in your chosen field, and the thought of whether this realization can be accomplished will come to you, and you will only think about it without doing anything. At this time, you must decide and act to overcome that thought and work hard.
  * **Step-4: High Incom Skills**: At this step, even though you are determined and working hard in your chosen field, if your financial difficulties are not resolved in that field, these financial weaknesses will prevent you from getting happiness from that field. Therefore, you should first identify whether the field you choose is a high-income field. But in any field, there may be some high-income fields that have not yet come out and are hidden, and finding them will take you to great heights.
  * **Step-5: Holistic View**: At this step, when you decide and work hard in your chosen field, there will be many sub-fields available within your chosen field. From there, you should choose the field that provides the highest income and decide and work hard in that field.
  * **Step-6: Role Model**: At this step, as you decide and work hard in your chosen field, you can achieve greater performance excellence by understanding the life model and work style of a person who has achieved high success in your chosen field and copying it in your life.
